Counterfeit Product Detection Using Blockchain

Blockchain technology provides a decentralized, immutable, and transparent ledger that ensures every product is uniquely registered. Manufacturers add product details to the blockchain, which can be verified at any point in the supply chain using a QR code. This helps in preventing counterfeit goods from entering the market, ensuring consumer trust and brand protection.

  • Immutability: Once recorded, data cannot be altered, ensuring product authenticity.
  • Transparency: Every transaction is visible and traceable.
  • Decentralization: No central authority controls the data, making it more secure.
  • QR Code Verification: Easy validation of product authenticity for consumers.

How It Works

Counterfeit Product Identification using Blockchain ensures product authenticity by securely storing unique product data on a tamper-proof distributed ledger.

Step 1: Manufacturer Registration

Manufacturers register products on the blockchain, generating a unique QR code for each item.

Step 2: Product Sale

Retailers and sellers verify the authenticity of products by scanning the QR code linked to blockchain data.

Step 3: Consumer Verification

Consumers scan the product's QR code to check its legitimacy using the blockchain record.
