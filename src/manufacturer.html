Manufacturer Process

The manufacturer plays a crucial role in ensuring product authenticity by registering products, sellers, and tracking distribution using blockchain.

Step 1: Register Product

The manufacturer registers a product on the blockchain, and a unique QR code is generated for it.

Step 2: Register Seller

The manufacturer adds trusted sellers to the blockchain to ensure genuine product distribution.

Step 3: Sell to Seller

The manufacturer transfers the product ownership to the registered seller via blockchain.

Step 4: View Sellers

The manufacturer can view and manage all registered sellers and their transactions.
